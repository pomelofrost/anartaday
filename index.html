An Art Per Day

The Metropolitan Museum contains 5,000 years of the most fascinating artworks worldwide. While it is important to visit the museum and witness the artworks in real life, the experience could be overwhelming. Therefore, An Art Per Day is a virtual space for people to view the artworks a new way. Pulling from the Met API, which is updated weekly and available on Github, visitors are able to focus on one artwork each day and view it in any distance.
